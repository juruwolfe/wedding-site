Other Accomodations

Downtown Boston

If you'd like to stay closer to the glittering metropolitan core, there are dozens of hotels in downtown Boston and the neighbouring towns.

If you intend to take transit to or from the rehearsal dinner or the Hotel Indigo (from which the wedding shuttle will depart), we recommend staying in proximity to the green D-Line on the T.

Newton or Dedham

If you are driving and want to stay out of the city, look for venues in Newton (to be close to the rehearsal dinner) or Dedham (to be close to the ceremony venue).

Finding hotels

We recommend Hotwire.com for low rates if you're not too picky about the exact venue and location.

AirBnB is a good option if you'd like to stay in a house.
